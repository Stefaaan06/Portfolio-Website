M I K

Mik is an atmospheric, narrative-driven platformer, set in a post-apocalyptic world. What was once a thriving megastructure is now a rotting corpse of a facility, where you find yourself exploring fragments of the past.

More Info

Features

  • Dark & Mysterious atmosphere
  • Narrative experience throughout a vast Megastructure
  • Classic parkour with environmental puzzles
  • Uncover the past and piece together the mysterious lore
  • Beautiful but optimised graphics
  • Jump, Wallrun and Dash through the game...
  • ...or take it slow and enjoy the view
