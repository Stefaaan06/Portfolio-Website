Akio

Akio is a fast paced, 2D platformer focused on speedrunning created for ScoreSpace2024 gamejam in 3 days.

Features

  • Speedrunning: This game is all about being fast.
  • Advanced Movement: Complicated and interesting combinations of different features can lead to your next good run
  • Competitive: Chain abilities, kill enemies and be the best
  • Leaderboard: Global Leaderboard
