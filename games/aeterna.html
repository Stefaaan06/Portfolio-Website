Aeterna

Embark on a non-Euclidean odyssey in Aeterna, a puzzle-parkour game set in an endlessly looping world. Explore mind-bending landscapes, solving puzzles, and going trough parkour challenges.

This is a game developed for GithubGameoff 2023 - Placed 56 overall and 26 in graphics out of 630 submissions.

Features

  • Infinite Exploration: Discover an ever-repeating world, bending reality in all directions.
  • Puzzles and Parkour: Challenge your mind and agility with puzzles and parkour.
  • OST: 5 unique Tracks
  • Unique Artstyle: Experience a visually stunning world with a distinctive art style.
