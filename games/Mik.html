M I K

Mik is a story based & fast-paced first person jump & run game in which you explore a detailed & interactable abandoned facility. Run on walls, jump over cliffs and find out about the past of Revelo Robotics.

Gameplay

Escape a broken & seemingly abandoned Facility by stringing together various movement abilities, interacting with the environment and solving simple Puzzles.
Traverse various Areas of the fallen Company and find out about its Past.

Features

  • Interactable Objects & Screens
  • Fast paced gameplay
  • Detailed & immersive levels
  • Singleplayer Story
  • Puzzles
  • Global Leaderboards
